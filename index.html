Buades Printershop (Spain)

Book Layout Designer - Management Assistant• From 2010 to 2013 (part time)

The best job I had. In this Printshop, I was in charge of the layout of books. It was my responsibility to fix the rags, widows and orphans, pagination and typography. I would, for example, check that all the chapter begun on the left side, the typo size was comfortable enough to read but also enough to make the book "affordable". I would also make the templates for the machine that would print the books. If all my colleges were busy, I would also help with graphic design, like fixing old pictures or making presentation cards. At some point, my boss wanted me to help with the management and bureaucracy. I had to leave this job because I moved to Sweden! I still visit them when I'm back in Spain.
